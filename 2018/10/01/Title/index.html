无题

发表于 2018-10-01 | 分类于 随笔

当你陷进生活里的时候，你也许难以察觉到大多数事物已经被你忽略，直到有一天你忽然发现它们已经对你产生了深远的影响。然后你也许会大叫一声：“噢，我都干了些什么！”

When you get stuck in the reality, you may hardly realise that most of the things are just ignored by you, until one day you suddenly discover they have laid a deep effect on you. And then you might shout aloud, “Oh, what have I done!”

我仍没有办法越过那道坎，但我该和他们和解了。因为，无论如何，他们已经真切地成为了我的一部分。这是事实。他们造就了我。

I still can’t find a way to get over it, but it’s time for me to make peace with them. Because, however, they have already become a real part of me. That’s a fact. They made me.

人生不如意事十之八九。

Life is not always a comfortable ride:)
